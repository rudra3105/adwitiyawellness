Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

We recommend arriving 15–20 minutes before your scheduled appointment. This gives you time to check in, complete a brief wellness consultation form, change into your robe and slippers, and begin your relaxation in our lounge before the treatment begins.

We provide complimentary robes, towels, and slippers for all guests. We suggest wearing comfortable, loose clothing to and from the spa. For body wraps and massage treatments, you will be professionally draped at all times during your treatment to ensure your comfort and privacy.

Absolutely. We offer specially adapted prenatal massage from the second trimester onwards (14+ weeks), performed by our certified prenatal therapist. Please inform us of your pregnancy when booking and note which trimester you are in. Certain treatments such as deep tissue massage, chemical peels, and body wraps with heat are not recommended during pregnancy.

We kindly request a minimum of 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will be charged 50% of the service fee. No-shows will be charged 100% of the treatment fee. We understand that emergencies happen and will do our best to accommodate you on a case-by-case basis.

Yes! Our beautifully presented gift vouchers are available in any denomination or for specific treatments. They are perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, Mother's Day, and corporate gifting. Gift vouchers can be purchased at the spa, over the phone, or online, and can be emailed as a digital copy or posted as a luxury physical card. Vouchers are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all guests in our dedicated car park behind the building. Simply inform the receptionist upon arrival and a valet attendant will be with you shortly. Street parking is also available on Tranquil Gardens Boulevard. We are also conveniently located a 5-minute walk from Beverly Hills Metro Station.

We exclusively use COSMOS-certified organic, cruelty-free products sourced from world-renowned brands including Eminence Organics, Elemis, and our own Adwitiya private blend essential oil collection. All products are free from parabens, synthetic fragrances, and harsh chemicals. We will always conduct a patch test for guests with sensitive skin or known allergies before beginning any treatment.
