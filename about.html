Our Beginning

A Vision Born from Passion

✦

Serenity Spa & Wellness was founded in 2012 with a single, unwavering belief: that true luxury is not about opulence — it's about feeling completely at peace. Our founder, Isabella Laurent, a certified holistic wellness practitioner and world traveler, spent a decade studying ancient healing traditions across Thailand, Bali, Japan, and Morocco before bringing the finest of those traditions home.

What began as an intimate boutique studio with just three treatment rooms has blossomed into a full-scale wellness destination with 18 specialist treatment rooms, a hydrotherapy suite, a couples retreat wing, and a meditation garden. Yet through every expansion, our philosophy has remained constant: each guest is treated as the only guest.

Today, Serenity is recognized as one of the region's most celebrated luxury spa destinations, having welcomed over 4,800 guests and maintained a 98% satisfaction rating — a testament to our uncompromising dedication to excellence.

Our Approach

The Serenity Philosophy

Four pillars that define our holistic approach to wellness.

I

Presence

We create environments and experiences that draw you fully into the present moment — away from the noise, into the now.

II

Purity

From the air you breathe to the products on your skin — everything we use is clean, natural, and purposefully chosen for your wellbeing.

III

Personalization

No two bodies are the same. Every treatment is adapted, every product selected, every pressure calibrated specifically for you.

IV

Permanence

We don't just treat symptoms. We teach lasting wellness habits and rituals that continue to serve you long after you leave our doors.
